Book Description

After her closest friend and cousin Abigail was turned down by the gentry after it was discovered that her father was a bigamist, Lady Jessica Archer lost interest in the dazzling pleasure of romance. Jessica views him as being wholly unfit, especially after he declares his intention to marry her while they are still hardly known.
